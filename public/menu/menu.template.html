How To Play

Start a game

Enter number of players and click start game

Make a move

Make a claim or challenge the last claim

Claim

Make a bid on total number of X dice with Y face between all players

The number of dice claimed must be greater than the previous claim

Choose any number of dice to put onto the board and reroll the remaining

Challenge

If you don't believe the previous claim is true, you may challenge and the dice will be counted
